My Favorite Mountain Ranges

The Sierras

Jagged as a saw, the Sierra Nevadas are my home turf. The first words I learned in Spanish were snowy and mountain range. Home to the highest mountain in the Continental U.S.

The Enchantments

Don't let their size fool you. At only 15 miles wide, this range is small but rugged. The reflection of Dragontail Peak on a still morning will take your breath away.

The Alps

Renown for their sweeping scenery, public-friendly sky lifts, and technical climbing routes. At least one visit here is worth the crowds.
